I was born in Michigan, lived in Illinois as a small child and then ended up back in Michigan for most of my life. I graduated from Rockford High School. Then I studied all things computers in college at Grand Rapids Community College and Ferris State University. I am currently employed at Harris Computer where I work on a property tax application called Aumentum. I mainly work on calculations that keep track of values on people's homes, but I do other stuff as well.

For my hobbies, I will do tutorials and read books on programming. I also try to keep my mind in shape by learning Spanish and my body in shape by exercising. If I get a chance, I have an interest in electronics and will build some circuits, or I will try to learn something in Adobe.
